What is this?

The Invasive Species Detector allows you to upload images of plants, animals, or insects that you suspect may be an invasive species. The Invasive Species Detector will give notify you if the subject is an invasive species or not!

How does it work?

This detector uses AI image classification to process images and return objects in that image. The AI has been trained on a large number of images of each invasive species in order to accurately identify them.

Tips for uploads:

  • Ensure good lighting of the subject
  • Higher resolution images produce better results
  • Try to isolate the subject from surrounding objects
